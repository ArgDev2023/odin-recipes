Butter Scones

These English Style Scones bake up light, soft, and fluffy, and are a wonderful treat for breakfast or afternoon tea.
Spread them with jam, clotted cream, butter, or simply eat them plain. Theyâ€™re incredibly delicious and are made in 25 minutes. No chilling required!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (10 ounces by weight)
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter at room temperature
  • 2/3 cup whole milk
  • 1 large egg
